two =_Task Wiki Open -- A script to facilitate working with task wiki files._

Usage: two [-a|-c|-h|-i|-l] [[attribute:]value] [filter]

  no args -- opens taskwiki root index.wiki
  no "attribute:" -- attribute=project

  -a [filter] -- info for project.any:

  -c [[attribute:]value] [filter] -- info using current context (default is to ignore)

  -h -- show two-help.txt (this file)

  -i [[attribute:]value] [filter] -- information about <value>

  -l -- to list wiki files
